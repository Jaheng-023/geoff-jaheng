Me, Myself and I

By: Geoffrey Estabalaya


My name is Geoffrey Estabalaya. I was born on September 23, 2004 in a hospital
maternal and newborn tertiary hospital located in Santa Cruz, Manila.
Currently living with my mother and aunt in Silvertown II, Malagasang 1-E.


I enjoy pursuing my happiness without causing harm to others.
I like animals, especially cats, as I find them to be the cutest creatures on the planet.
Being an ambivert, I enjoy both being by myself and around people at different times.


I like basketball, but I don't play it very often.
I am an ambivert, so I used to play a lot of online games and made it a habit.
I recently realized that I enjoy writing, especially light or web novels; the pictures above are drafts of my novels.
