My Favorite

by Geoffrey Estabalaya

"My favorite color was White because I found it minimal and associated to cleanliness."

"Adobo has been my favorite food since I was a child because my aunt has been cooking it

for me ever since then."

"Vanilla and taro ice cream has been my favorite dessert since I was a child."

"Even though I've disliked math and all associated topics since I was a child, as I

got older, I started liking math and it eventually became one of my favorite subjects."

"I like hip-hop music because it makes me want to dance, although I'm not sure why."

"Not only do I enjoy the film Maze Runner, but I also enjoy the Maze Runner movie series."

"My favorite anime is "One Piece," and I adore the entire story and the series as a whole.

Anything related to "One Piece" makes me happy."

"The television show Lucifer is my personal favorite. I know this seems strange coming from a deeply

religious household, but the show hasn't made me any less of a believer in God."
