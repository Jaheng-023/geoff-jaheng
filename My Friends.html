My Friends

By: Geoffrey Estabalaya

"Although I don't have many friends, I can tell you that the ones I will name are trustworthy friends.

As I write this, I have been friends with "The Boys" for around seven years, having met them when I was still in high school.

Our shared interests have allowed us to become a close group of friends, along with my other pals. Jerome, Lloyd, and Jay Carlo are the three members of the group with whom I was closest.

I am currently attending Cavite State University - Imus Campus alongside Jerome and Lloyd, while the other is attending a separate collage."

"I've met a lot of people as I've grown and pursued my degree in fashion, and I can call some of them friends. "
