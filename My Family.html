My family

By: Geoffrey Estabalaya


My mother Socorro Estabalaya and my two aunts, Julia and Cecille Estabalaya, are the ones who raised me.

>In 2017, my Tita Cecille passed away already.


My mother was solo parent and with my two aunties they guided me to become what I am now.

I am grateful because they teach me to respect. be good person and many more.

My extended family is from the United States and the Philippines.

My family is supportive and keeps each other informed all the time, and that is how I grew up.
